About Dev Owl Inc.

Mission & Vision

The value of life is important, thought what is life? To put it simply, life is time. A person’s life is a collection of seconds, and the seconds that flee are never coming back. I was always fascinated about time and its beautiful secret. Time tells a lot of stories, yet its so mysterious. As I grew up and became the man I am today, I could not ignore this fact any longer. I want to help people, help people enjoy their time, those beautiful seconds. I want to make people’s lives easier, faster, and more convenient. I am learning everyday and every second for this mission. My mission, is to increase the number of tasks a person can do in one sitting. This way, people will have more seconds to enjoy, rather than spending most of their seconds doing tasks. This is why I am keen to provide my humble services, and learn more to develop more solutions to people’s daily problems. I want to change frustrations into smiles, one line of code at a time, that is my vision.

Thank you, and I hope you have a good day.

Sincerely,

Ibrahim Mohamed.
